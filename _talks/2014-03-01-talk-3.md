Talk on using deep learning models like feedforward neural networks, LSTM and Transformer to predict fatigue life for various alloys with the motivation of developing a universal fatigue life prediction model. Also included workflow for converting literature results on multiaxial fatigue life tests into time-series data for model training and testing.
